# Drivers

The MIND SDK provides multiple drivers for you to connect your input and output to the HEXA robot. To add a driver, just add it to the `import` statement in your golang file for your Skill. Here are the currently available drivers:

* [accelerometer](/APIReference/accelerometer.md)
* [adc](/APIReference/adc.md)
* [audio](/APIReference/audio.md)
* [distance](/APIReference/distance.md)
* [gpio](/APIReference/gpio.md)
* [hexabody](/APIReference/hexabody.md)
* [i2c](/APIReference/i2c.md)
* [infrared](/APIReference/infrared.md)
* [media](/APIReference/media.md)
